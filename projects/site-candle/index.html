Stages of creating candles

Creating a soy wax candle can consist of the following steps:

  1. Preparation of materials: small fragments of soy wax, candle wick, fragrances, dyes and other additional materials.

  2. Mould preparation: choosing a candle mould and preparing it for filling with wax.

  3. Wax preparation: melting soy wax in a melting device to the desired temperature. Add fragrances and colourants as required.

  4. Preparing the wick: inserting the wick into the mould and fixing it so that it is evenly tensioned.

  5. Preparation of materials: small fragments of soy wax, candle wick, fragrances, dyes and other additional materials.

  6. Filling the mould: Pour the wax into the mould until it is completely filled. Wait for it to harden.
